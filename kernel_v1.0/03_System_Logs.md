# 03 — System Logs (Case studies)

Purpose
- Present anonymized, de-identified, or fictionalized case studies to illustrate concepts, pitfalls, and lessons.

Case study template
- Title
- Context and baseline
- Intervention / timeline
- Outcomes
- Lessons learned
- References

Sample (fictionalized)
- "Marilyn — stabilization through community and care coordination" (summary)
- "Prince — complexities of polypharmacy and the importance of pharmacogenomics in clinical decision-making" (summary)

Notes
- All case studies must be reviewed for privacy and ethical compliance before publication. Real-world cases require explicit consent or must be sufficiently de-identified.
