Contributing to Recovery Kernel

Thank you for your interest in contributing. Please follow these guidelines:

- Issues & PRs: Open issues to discuss significant changes. Submit pull requests against the main branch only after discussion unless small fixes.
- Code style & docs: Keep markdown, docs, and code well-formatted. Add references where appropriate.
- Protocol contributions: Include intended audience, evidence level, contraindications, and citations for clinical or safety-related content.
- Review & consent: Any real-world case studies or data must have explicit consent or be fully de-identified.

Community conduct
- Be respectful and cite evidence. Avoid making prescriptive medical claims without peer-reviewed support. If in doubt, suggest editorial review by qualified clinicians.

Security & sensitive content
- Do not include private health data or personally identifiable information in contributions.
